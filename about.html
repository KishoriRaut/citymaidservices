About City Maid

City Maid is a platform dedicated to connecting trusted domestic help with employers in Nepal. We understand the importance of finding reliable household assistance and aim to make this process as smooth and secure as possible.

Our mission is to create a safe and efficient marketplace where both domestic workers and employers can find their perfect match. We believe in providing equal opportunities and fair treatment for all parties involved.

Our Features

Verified Profiles

All profiles are manually verified to ensure authenticity and reliability.

Secure Payments

Safe and secure payment processing through Khalti integration.

Easy Search

Advanced search and filter options to find the perfect match.

Direct Contact

Connect directly with potential matches after verification.

How It Works

1. Create a profile or post a job listing - it's completely free!

2. Browse through available maids or jobs that match your requirements.

3. Use our advanced filters to narrow down your search.

4. When you find a potential match, unlock their contact information through our secure payment system.

5. Connect directly and arrange the details of your working relationship.
